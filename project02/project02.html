Design Brief

The Goal of this project is promote and design different advertisements for the up in coming event presented by the Cal State Fullerton Art department. This event is called Activate and is a new event that if goes well, will be put on an anual basis. The objective of the event is to spark inpspiration and creativity for the mainly unused quad in the art department. As a design student every participants objective is to provide a cohesive collection of posters,logos, animations, and videos.

(1) Who is the audience?
The Audience is definitely aimed to youthful young adults who are passionate about the arts.

(2) What message needs to be communicated?
The message of the my designs ae to give the Activate Exhibit a poppy, punky, in your face outlook of what to expect and draw people into wanting to come check out the art community of Cal State Fullerton.

(3) What is the competition and marketplace?
list 5 websites

1. www.postermywall.com
2. www.lagunaartmuseum.org
3. www.commonthreadco.com
4. www.dtsaartwalk.org
5. www.creativecrusade.com

(4) What is the context?
The context is based off different students who go to Cal State Fullerton, and provide a humorous powerful, energetic message to want to create.

(5) In what voice?
The topic is inspired from punk culture in order to induce and energetic outlook with a youthful twist.
Fun, Punky, Vibrant, Exciting, and Poppy

(6) What kind of response is desired
he topic is based of what the youth might want to see and if they come to look at the campus and see art that is more energetic may be more inticed to get inspired.
