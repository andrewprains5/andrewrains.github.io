Design Brief

On this page I put together the process and designs for the Home page of my website. On my home page I want to portray myself as a graphic design porfessional that catches the eye of any potential employers in the future. I not only want to desplay my disign skils but also my artistic abilities to show my different skill sets and eye for artistically driven design.


(1) Who is the audience?
The Audience is or any potential employers or clients to get a first impression of my design skills and get a glipse into my personality.

(2) What message needs to be communicated?
The message I would like to convey is that I am a trained profeesional who is eager to work in the design feild and portrays cpabilities to give off the feeling that I understand what I am doing.


(3) What is the context?
The context will contain work I have developed and created, which include my artistic side of traditional fine arts and graphic side. It will include paintings, photography, 3d work, illustrations, and any ui ux graphic design work.

(4) In what voice?
The voice will be my voice and I would like to desplay as much of my personality as i can.
