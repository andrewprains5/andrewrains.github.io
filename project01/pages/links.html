Four Favorite App

  1. Google

    Category: Seach Engine

    Rating: ★★★★★

    Google Search, also referred to as Google Web Search or simply Google, is a web search engine developed by Google. It is the most used search engine on the World Wide Web across all platforms, with 92.62% market share as of June 2019, handling more than 5.4 billion searches each day.

  2. Youtube

    Category: Video Broadcasting

    Rating: ★★★★☆

    A YouTube channel description is the text on your channel's “About” page. It helps viewers understand what to expect from your brand. A YouTube video description is the text below each of your videos. It helps viewers find your content and decide whether to watch it.

  3. Instagram

    Category: Social Media App

    Rating: ★★★★★

    An Instagram bio is the small area underneath your username for you to share some details about yourself or your brand. Your Instagram bio can include a brief self or brand description, contact information, emojis, hashtags and more.

  4. Facebook

    Category: Social Network

    Rating: ★★★★☆

    Facebook is a popular free social networking website that allows registered users to create profiles, upload photos and video, send messages and keep in touch with friends, family and colleagues.
