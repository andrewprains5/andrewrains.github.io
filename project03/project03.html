Design Brief

When trying to incorperate motion into graphics it is not just about making an image, vector, illustration, etc, move from point A to point B, it is about making the subject matter move as though there is life behind it. Its about making a two dimensional object appear as though its living in a deeper plain other than a flat surface.

Process

1: Purpose and Audience

The purpose of this project was to produce a short video about Covid 19 and social distancing through an artistic insight. During this crazy time people are feeling anxiety and stress due to isolation as well as many other burdens caused by this pandemic. So through the schools competition and our class I wanted to develop a video that is fun loving and up beat to help people step back and maybe ease the stress going on currently. The intended audience for people of all ages. In the second video I collaberated with a friend in a sepereate class who wrote a covid song and illustrated a character that he needed help animating. So I helped him out and we created a nice funny video intended to be created for the reasons as the first video I made on my own.
