Design Brief

When trying to incorperate motion into graphics it is not just about making an image, vector, illustration, etc, move from point A to point B, it is about making the subject matter move as though there is life behind it. Its about making a two dimensional object appear as though its living in a deeper plain other than a flat surface.

About this event

A Special Project During the pandemics : Dean Dale Merrill is hosting a contest for College of the Arts students. Feed your need for creativity in these unprecedented times by creating a short, innovative video showcasing dance, music, theatre, or visual arts and you could win $500 in cash!
